<h1>TENET</h1>
Tenet is a web-based platform designed for people to connect, communicate, and collaborate around shared interests. Users can create or join rooms for various topics, share their thoughts through messages, images, and files, and even initiate video meetings within those rooms.

Whether it's one-on-one conversations or interest-based group chats with video calls and screen sharing,Tenet provides a unified, secure, and intuitive environment for meaningful interactions.
<hr>
<h2>Features</h2>
<li>User Authentication: Sign up and Sign in.</li>
<li>One-on-One Chats: Real-time messaging with individual users.</li>
<li>Group Rooms: Create or join interest-based rooms for group messaging.</li>
<li>Video Calling: Start 1:1 or group video calls with screen sharing.</li>
<li>Participants View: See a list of members in a group.</li>
<hr>
